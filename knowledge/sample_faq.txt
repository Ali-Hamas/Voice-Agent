Britsync Demo Clinic — Frequently Asked Questions

Opening hours
We are open Monday to Friday from 9am to 6pm. We are closed on Saturdays, Sundays, and UK bank holidays.

Location
We are located at 12 High Street, London, UK. The nearest tube station is a five minute walk away. Parking is available on the street and in the public car park behind the building.

Services offered
- General consultations
- Blood tests and basic diagnostics
- Vaccinations (flu, travel, childhood)
- Health screenings
- Minor procedures (mole removal, stitches, ear syringing)

Pricing
- Standard consultation: £80
- Extended consultation (30 minutes): £140
- Blood test panel: from £120
- Flu vaccination: £25
- Travel vaccination consultation: £50 (vaccines priced separately)

Booking an appointment
Appointments can be booked by phone during opening hours. We try to offer same-week appointments for non-urgent matters and same-day slots for urgent issues. New patients should arrive 10 minutes early to complete a short registration form.

Cancellation policy
Please give at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged 50% of the consultation fee.

Insurance
We accept self-pay and several private insurers including Bupa, AXA, Vitality, and Aviva. Please confirm with your insurer before the appointment that the visit is covered.

Prescriptions
Repeat prescriptions for existing patients can be requested by phone or email and are usually ready within 48 hours.

Languages
Our clinicians speak English, Bengali, Hindi, and Arabic. Please let us know your language preference when booking.

Emergencies
For medical emergencies, please call 999 immediately. We are not an emergency service.
