Set forwarding from the VoIP dashboard — 1 minute, no calls needed

  1. Log into the restaurant's phone provider dashboard (RingCentral, Vonage, Nextiva, Dialpad, 8x8, etc.).
  2. Open Phone Numbers or Call Handling & Forwarding.
  3. Pick the main business number.
  4. Change the incoming call rule from "Ring Phones" to "Forward to External Number".
  5. Paste this number: {{ tw }}
  6. Save changes. Done — every incoming call now goes to the AI agent.

No star codes, no carrier phone calls. Most VoIP providers also let you schedule forwarding on/off by time of day.

⚠️ 3 settings the AI needs your carrier to do right

  • Pass-through Caller ID — make sure the old carrier forwards the original customer's number, not the restaurant's own number. Otherwise the AI won't know who is calling and can't text them back.
  • Concurrent-call capacity — ask the old carrier how many forwarded calls the line can handle simultaneously. Standard landlines often only allow 1–2. If 5 customers ring at once, the 3rd will get busy. (Twilio itself handles thousands.)
  • Disable old voicemail — turn off the carrier's voicemail or set its delay to maximum. If the old voicemail picks up after 4 rings, it will cut off the AI before it can answer.

💡 After forwarding is on, dial the old number from your mobile — you should hear the AI agent.
